What's Social Engineering?

Social engineering is a type of psychological tactic used by attackers to trick victims into doing things they shouldn't do.

It relies on human error, rather than vulnerabilties in software and operating systems.

The goal of attackers is usually to get their victims to reveal sensitive information, such as login credentials, bank information, or computer access.
